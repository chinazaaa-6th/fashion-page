Fashion Lives Here!


Opulent designs and colours are emblematic characteristics of African fashion - known for being anything but boring. Historically, prints have always been the focus. These are rooted in tradition, harnessing original, native techniques in their curation, using local artisans. African fashion represents many different cultures and traditions, each with its own unique style. The diversity of African fashion is symbolic of Africa's diversity in culture, language, and ethnicity. More than that, African fashion shows emotion, style and even status. In West Africa, Nigeria takes the cup with their explicit fashion style and trend. Indeed, fashion lives in Africa.
